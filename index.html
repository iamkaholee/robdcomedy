ABOUT

Rob DeSantis is a Philadelphia Comedian and Writer who is a stand out comedian to watch in the Philadelphia area. Rob is a regular at the prestigious Philadelphia Helium Comedy Club and has opened for such national headliners as Dave Attell, Charlie Murphy, Brian Posehn, Chelsea Peretti, and Tom Green (just to name a few). Rob is an active member of the Philadelphia comedy community, and works in many clubs up and down the east coast.

"
ROB'S COMEDY STYLE RANGES FROM SEMI-CLEAN TO DIRTY AS HELL AS DEPENDING ON THE AUDIENCE

In addition to Stand Up, Rob is a contributing author to multiple comedy websites, including comedy news site The Global News Network, as well as humor site 2 Kilograms, and has authored articles which have gone viral (over 10,000 hits in the first 24 hours). Additionally, Rob was co-host of 'Bob & Dave Are Terrible People' which aired live every First and Third Wednesday for over 2 years, part of the Laffcast network at: www.laffcast.com
